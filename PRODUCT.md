# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary users are casual ravers and hobby jewelry makers who want a fast, playful way to design kandi-style bracelet patterns and share their creations with friends.

## Product Purpose

Kandi helps people create and iterate on digital bracelet designs in a tactile editor, then publish and share those designs with others. Success means users can quickly make something personal, feel proud of the result, and easily send it to their community.

## Brand Personality

ASCII, refined, professional. The experience should feel crisp, intentional, and reliable while staying approachable for creative play.

## Anti-references

Avoid anything that reads as AI slop: generic gradient-heavy SaaS templates, repetitive card-grid UI patterns, and visual choices that feel uncrafted or interchangeable.

## Design Principles

1. Make creation feel immediate: keep editing actions direct, with low friction between idea and visible change.
2. Keep craft visible: typography, spacing, and controls should look deliberate, not templated.
3. Balance play and precision: support expressive outcomes while preserving predictable behavior.
4. Share with confidence: publishing and sharing states should be clear, trustworthy, and calm.
5. Preserve focus: keep the editor shell quiet enough that the bracelet design remains the hero.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard-first interaction patterns where practical, preserve clear focus visibility, ensure robust contrast, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
